In the town of Hunter’s Run, in a land not entirely unlike our own, a young boy named Strider longs for more. He was raised to be a blacksmith, like his father, yet he dreams of adventure and knowledge and grand endeavors.

But on a cool fall afternoon, everything changes… And Strider makes a friend that changes his world forever...

Strider can't believe his eyes. "A dragon!"

Dragons are extremely rare, and Strider has never seen one. Strider has several drakes as pets - small creatures that look like dragons, but are nothing compared to this amazing creature. Dragons are wild! Dragon poachers roam the land, hunting them for their valuable hides and mysterious, said to be magical, properties.

"Hey, you’re hurt," said Strider, noticing the creature's wound. "Let me help."

Strider stops short. "Dragon poachers!"

"Sorry dragon, but your time is up," the dragon poacher hisses.

Looking at the majestic creature writhing in pain, Strider knows he has to do something...

"No!" Strider shouts. "Leave him alone."

"Get out of the way, boy," snarls the dragon poacher.

"I will NOT!" replies Strider fearlessly.

He stands firm, the poachers eyeing him. And then...

The poachers turn and leave.

"Don't worry," Strider says, examining the wound. "I should be able to fix you up. I have all sorts of books on biology and medicine."

Strider pulls the arrow from the creature's wing. The dragon moans in pain.

"I even know a little about dragon anatomy. Just don't tell anyone, OK?" Strider says, with a soft laugh. "Everyone thinks I'm weird enough as it is."

"I think I'll call you... Arrow..." Strider says.

From that moment on, Strider and Arrow are inseparable. As time passes, they develop an undying loyalty to one another. Strider vows that he will do anything for Arrow.

And soon, he will.
