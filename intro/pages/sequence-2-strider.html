Arrow has difficulty breathing. His stomach is racked with searing pain and his breath reeks of ammonia.

Strider, sick with worry, fears his best friend will die.

And no one can help – even the local animal doctor knows little about the diseases that plague dragons.

With nowhere else to go, Strider visits the town elder, who tells him to take Arrow to the Drake Breeders’ Guild.

“What do drakes have to do with dragons?” Strider asks.

“Drakes and dragons are similar in many ways,” the town elder tells him. “The Drake Breeders’ Guild studies and breeds drakes so that they might learn more about the illnesses that plague dragons.”

Early in the morning, with the grass still wet and the sky a midnight blue, Strider and Arrow set out in search of the famed Drake Breeders’ Guild.

It is a perilous land, with danger at every turn, and the journey is long and hard.

Days stretch into weeks and weeks stretch into months. Arrow’s health worsens. Strider knows his friend will not last much longer.

But finally, when Strider fears they can travel no further...

They arrive at The Valley – the home of the Drake Breeder’s Guild. It is the most magnificent sight Strider has ever seen.

It is there that Strider will learn the fate of his friend...
