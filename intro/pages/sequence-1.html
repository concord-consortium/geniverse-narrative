In the town of Hunter’s Run, in a land not entirely unlike our own, a young girl named Scarlett longs for more. She was raised to be a blacksmith, like her father, yet she dreams of adventure and knowledge and grand endeavors.

But on a cool fall afternoon, everything changes… And Scarlett makes a friend that changes her world forever...

Scarlett can't believe her eyes. "A dragon!"

Dragons are extremely rare, and Scarlett has never seen one. Scarlett has several drakes as pets - small creatures that look like dragons, but are nothing compared to this amazing creature. Dragons are wild! Dragon poachers roam the land, hunting them for their valuable hides and mysterious, said to be magical, properties.

"Hey, you’re hurt," said Scarlett, noticing the creature's wound. "Let me help."

Scarlett stops short. "Dragon poachers!"

"Sorry dragon, but your time is up," the dragon poacher hisses.

Looking at the majestic creature writhing in pain, Scarlett knows she has to do something...

"No!" Scarlett shouts. "Leave him alone."

"Get out of the way, girl," snarls the dragon poacher.

"I will NOT!" replies Scarlett fearlessly.

She stands firm, the poachers eyeing her. And then...

The poachers turn and leave.

"Don't worry," Scarlett says, examining the wound. "I should be able to fix you up. I have all sorts of books on biology and medicine."

Scarlett pulls the arrow from the creature's wing. The dragon moans in pain.

"I even know a little about dragon anatomy. Just don't tell anyone, OK?" Scarlett says, with a soft laugh. "Everyone thinks I'm weird enough as it is."

"I think I'll call you... Arrow..." Scarlett says.

From that moment on, Scarlett and Arrow are inseparable. As time passes, they develop an undying loyalty to one another. Scarlett vows that she will do anything for Arrow.

And soon, she will.
