In the Valley, Strider and Arrow are met by Dr. Weaver, who sternly tells them, “This is the Drake Breeders’ guild, not ‘fix the little boys’ dragon guild.’”

“But you study drakes to HELP dragons, right? You have to help Arrow, please!” Strider begs. “He’s all I have. We’ve come so far.”

Dr. Weaver turns to walk away.

“I can help!” Strider said, “I can learn! Please – you don't know what it’s like – I can’t lose my best friend!”

Dr. Weaver stops, then pauses for a long moment before answering. “You start work in the laboratory first thing in the morning.”

At the crack of dawn, Strider goes to see Dr. Weaver in his laboratory. He can’t believe what he sees there.

“Wow,” Strider's eyes were as wide as saucers. “What's all this?”

“This is the laboratory,” says the Doctor curtly. “This is where we investigate drake DNA, run breeding experiments and solve genetic mysteries.”

Strider nods, trying to cover his confusion. He understands only a fraction of what Dr. Weaver says, but he is sure he will learn.

“Dr. Weaver,” Strider asks, finally, “Why did you change your mind?”

“Because you were wrong,” Dr. Weaver replies. “I do know what it’s like to lose a best friend.”

And with that, Strider goes to work - experimenting, learning, and investigating. He will learn about and breed drakes – studying them, analyzing them, and applying his new knowledge to figure out what was causing Arrow's illness.

His adventure has just begun, and he knows there is no turning back. He will not allow himself to fail...
